What is Performance Testing?

Performance Testing is performed to determe how well a system performs in terms of responsiveness and stability.

I use Blazemeter to test performance. We will be able to ensure that you website can handle at least 1,000 virtual users. Blazemeter measures hits, responsiveness, and latency. If any errors occur, Blazemeter will include those in the summary report.
